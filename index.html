Introduction

DoseGuard ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

By using DoseGuard, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Child profiles (name, date of birth, weight, allergies)
  • Medication information (name, dosage, frequency, schedule)
  • Dose logs (timestamp, caregiver, notes)
  • Caregiver profiles (name, role)
  • Contact information when you submit feedback (name, email, message)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App usage data (features used, crash reports)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal health information with third parties
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect browsing history
  • We do not use your data for advertising

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the medication tracking service
  • To send medication reminders and notifications
  • To prevent duplicate dosing through safety alerts
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To respond to your support requests and feedback

Data Storage and Security

All medication and child data is stored locally on your device. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. This does not include:

  • Trusted third parties who assist us in operating the app (subject to confidentiality agreements)
  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • Other caregivers you explicitly invite to share tracking for a child

Children's Privacy

DoseGuard is designed for use by parents and caregivers to track medications for children. We understand the sensitive nature of children's health data and take extra precautions to safeguard it. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All data is entered and managed by adult caregivers.
